    • Meet Our Chefs

    Vikas Khanna

    Vikas Khanna is an award-winning Michelin starred Indian food chef. He has worked for the Taj, Oberoi, Welcome group, and Leela Group of Hotels and with some of the most influential chefs of the world letting in Gordon Ramsay, Bobby Flay, Jean-Georges Vongerichten.

    Susanna Foo

    Susanna has been acknowledged as one of the best Chinese chefs and hailed as an innovator by Gourmet, Food & Wine, Esquire and The New York Times. With two cookbooks and two James Beard Awards, Foo is highly decorated in the food world and knows that altering the ordinary is the key to success.

    Rick Bayless

    Rick Bayless is an American chef who specializes in traditional Mexican cuisine with modern nterpretations.Most people know Rick Bayless from winning the title of Bravo’s Top Chef Masters, beating out the French and Italian with his authentic Mexican cuisine.

    Nicole Gomes

    Her passion for food has taken her around the globe, earning her a place alongside some of Canada’s best chefs and as a contestant on Top Chef Canada. Let Nicole share her knowledge and passion in the best way possible, by having her and her team cook for you.
